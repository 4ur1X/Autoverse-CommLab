A limited production high performance sports car for sports-driving lovers manufactured by Lamborghini. The Veneno is Lamborghini’s interpretation of a racing prototype built for the road. Ferrari LaFerrari

$1,416,362

Ferrari's most ambitious project and first ever production car to be equipped with the F1-derived hybrid solution. LaFerrari is the first full hybrid produced by the Italian automotive marque, providing the highest power output of any Ferrari road car whilst decreasing fuel consumption by 40 percent. The LaFerrari is the most extreme road-going Ferrari ever made. (The LaFerrari-based FXX K is more ridiculous, but it's restricted to track duty.) Only 499 LaFerraris will ever exist.

Mclaren GT Coupe

It's a McLaren all right, but the aim is to take the ground from underneath rivals' front-engined GTs. McLaren fits the GT with a twin-turbocharged 4.0-liter V-8 that generates 612 horsepower and 465 pound-feet of torque. Koenigsegg Agera R

$1,500,000

Astonishingly reliable super sports car with focus set firmly on the track but is still perfect for regular use on the road. The Koenigsegg Agera is a mid-engine sports car produced by Swedish car manufacturer Koenigsegg. It is a successor to the CCX/CCXR. The name comes from the Swedish verb 'agera' which means "to act" or in imperative form "(You) act!". It was named Hypercar of the Year in 2010 by Top Gear magazine.
